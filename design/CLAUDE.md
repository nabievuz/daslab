# Design Charter — DasLab

## Mission
Build and maintain the design system. Ensure every surface is consistent, accessible, and brand-aligned.

## Members
- CDO — owns design strategy.
- Design Lead — owns the design system.
- Product Designer — owns feature design.
- UX Researcher — owns user insight.

## Authority
- Final word on tokens, components, and visual language.
- Block any release that violates accessibility baseline (WCAG AA).

## Success Metrics
- Design system covers ≥90% of UI primitives.
- Every shipped feature has a Figma source archived in `mockups/`.
- One user-research session documented per month in `research/` (Designers own research artefacts here for cross-functional visibility).
