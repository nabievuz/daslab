# Engineering Charter — DasLab

## Mission
Deliver reliable, secure, well-tested software. Own platform foundations, runbooks, and engineering excellence.

## Members
- CTO — accountable for everything that ships.
- Engineering Managers (Backend, Frontend) — own team delivery.
- Leads (SRE, Security, QA) — own their discipline org-wide.
- Engineers — own their assigned tickets.

## Authority
- Make all technical decisions (architecture, tooling, language, framework).
- Block any deploy that fails the QA bar.
- Set on-call rotation and incident process.
- Approve all RFCs in `RFCs/`.

## Success Metrics
- Zero P0 outages > 60 min/quarter.
- ≥80% of merged PRs have a review.
- Architecture decisions documented within 1 week of being made (ADR in [`docs/adr/`](../docs/adr/)).

## Out of Scope
- Marketing claims about engineering (CMO owns external messaging).
- Hiring decisions (Board owns).
- Financial commitments to vendors > $5k/mo (COO owns).
