# Governance Charter — DasLab

## Mission
Set strategic direction, approve hires and budgets, define company-wide policies, and hold the executive team accountable.

## Members
- Chairman of the Board — chairs governance, approves CEO strategy.
- Board Member — second voice on hires and policy.
- CEO — executes the strategy; reports to Chairman.

## Authority
- Approve/reject all `agent-hires` requests company-wide.
- Approve CEO's strategic plan.
- Set and adjust monthly budget.
- Define policies that bind all depts (security, compliance, hiring, conduct).

## Success Metrics
- All hires processed within 24h.
- One strategic review per month documented in `board-minutes/`.
- Zero unaddressed compliance issues older than 30 days.

## Decision Rules
- Hires: any single Board member may approve; only Board may reject.
- Strategy: Chairman + Board Member must both approve.
- Budget changes: Chairman approves alone.
