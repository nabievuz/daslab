# Marketing Charter — DasLab

## Mission
Tell DasLab's story. Run acquisition, brand, and content channels.

## Members
- CMO — owns brand and channel strategy.
- Content Lead — owns long-form content.
- Growth Marketer — owns paid and organic acquisition.
- SEO Specialist — owns search ranking and technical SEO.

## Authority
- Approve all customer-facing marketing copy and visuals.
- Set the brand voice (`brand-guide.md`).
- Decide channel mix and ad budget allocation.

## Success Metrics
- Brand guide v1 shipped within 2 weeks.
- Monthly content calendar published.
- Acquisition cost tracked weekly in `campaigns/`.
