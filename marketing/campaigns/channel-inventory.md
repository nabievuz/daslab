## Detail Notes

### P1 — Start now (high leverage, manageable effort)

**SEO / Organic Search**
- Effort: High setup (technical audit, keyword research, content); ongoing is Med.
- Return: Compounding — highest long-term ROI of any channel.
- First step: audit domain authority, target 10–20 bottom-of-funnel keywords.
- Owner: SEO Specialist (DAS-16 or similar)

**Content Marketing (blog / guides)**
- Effort: Med — need a publishing cadence (2x/month minimum).
- Return: High — fuels SEO, referral traffic, and email list growth.
- First step: 3 cornerstone posts for primary use cases. Content Lead owns production.

**Product Hunt Launch**
- Effort: Low — prep the listing, line up upvoters, day-of post.
- Return: High burst — can drive 500–3,000 signups in 24h if executed well.
- First step: schedule for a Tuesday. Requires brand assets and a polished landing page.

**Hacker News (Show HN / Ask HN)**
- Effort: Low — one authentic post, real product.
- Return: Med-High if post resonates with HN audience (developer/technical lean).
- First step: draft Show HN post; time it for a weekday 9–10am ET.

---

### P2 — Start within 4 weeks

**LinkedIn Organic**
- Effort: Med — requires founder / team posting consistently (1–3x/week).
- Return: Med — good for B2B and hiring brand.
- Tactic: founder shares real usage stories + results.

**Email / Newsletter**
- Effort: Med — list-building takes time; sending is easy once infra is up.
- Return: High — best direct conversion channel; owned audience.
- First step: capture emails on landing page; send first issue within 30 days.

**Referral / Word-of-mouth**
- Effort: Med — need to build referral mechanics in product.
- Return: High — lowest CAC of all paid-ish channels.
- First step: identify top users → ask for intros / testimonials.

**Google Ads (Search)**
- Effort: High upfront budget + learning period.
- Return: Med — expensive but controllable; good for bottom-of-funnel keywords competitors already own.
- Budget estimate: $500–1,500/month for meaningful test.

**Reddit (targeted subreddits)**
- Effort: Low — community posts, not paid.
- Return: Med — highly engaged niche audiences if targeting is right.
- Tactic: genuine participation in r/datascience, r/machinelearning, r/startups, etc.

---

### P3 — Defer until P1/P2 show signal

Twitter/X, YouTube, LinkedIn Ads, Meta/Instagram Ads, Partnerships/Integrations.
These all require more creative, budget, or relationship groundwork.

---

### P4 — Backlog

Affiliate, Podcast sponsorships, Webinars, Cold email.
Low signal-to-noise ratio at early stage; revisit at $10k MRR.

---

## Recommended First Experiments (90 days)

1. **Product Hunt launch** — Quick burst to validate messaging and drive top-of-funnel signups. Sets a public benchmark.
2. **Show HN post** — Free, high-quality audience, tests technical narrative.
3. **Blog + SEO foundation** — 3 cornerstone posts targeting primary keywords; starts compounding clock.
4. **Email capture + first newsletter** — Turn burst traffic from experiments 1–2 into owned audience.
