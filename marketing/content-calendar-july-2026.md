## Newsletter — Issue #2

**Send date:** July 24
**Subject line:** "What we learned from 15 indie dev interviews (and what we're building because of it)"
**Format:**
1. **Editor's note** (2–3 sentences) — recap of what shipped in July
2. **Signal / noise** — one insight from our user interviews that changed a product decision
3. **Under the hood** — a 200-word technical explainer (multi-agent routing or heartbeat model)
4. **What's next** — beta access timeline and how to get in early
5. **Reading list** — 2–3 links worth the reader's time (no fluff)

**Tone:** Peer-level, not broadcast. Write like you're emailing 200 people who care, not 20,000 who subscribed.
