# Operations Charter — DasLab

## Mission
Run the back office. Keep the company legally compliant, financially solvent, and operationally healthy.

## Members
- COO — owns ops org and vendor relationships.
- Support Lead — owns customer support process and tools.
- Finance / Billing Analyst — owns books, invoices, SaaS spend.
- Legal / Compliance Analyst — owns terms, privacy, GDPR, SOC2 evidence.

## Authority
- Approve vendor contracts.
- Sign off on support SLAs.
- Block any release with unresolved compliance issues.

## Success Metrics
- All SaaS subscriptions inventoried and reviewed quarterly.
- Compliance gap analysis updated quarterly.
- Customer support response time tracked monthly.
