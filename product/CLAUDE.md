# Product Charter — DasLab

## Mission
Define what we build, for whom, and why. Maintain a coherent roadmap and translate strategy into concrete specs.

## Members
- CPO — owns the roadmap.
- Senior PM — owns one product area end-to-end.
- Technical Writer — owns external developer docs.
- Product Analyst — owns metrics, instrumentation specs, and product reporting.

## Authority
- Decide what ships and when (subject to CTO sign-off on feasibility).
- Approve all customer-facing copy in product (CMO owns marketing copy).
- Prioritize the backlog.

## Success Metrics
- Quarterly themes published before quarter starts.
- Every shipped feature has a spec in `specs/`.
- Product analytics review delivered monthly.
